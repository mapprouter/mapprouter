Posted on 2022-02-19

Yesterday, from the hot search list of microblog, I saw an interesting topic: what is high-quality sleep? When I opened it, I found that this topic was related to Gu ailing. She shared the secret of her success, that is, how to make her sleep for 10 hours every day.
As the saying goes, laymen watch the excitement while experts watch the doorway. A key word extracted from this topic is sleep, and a big demand derived from sleep is insomnia.

01

Do so many people really have insomnia? I don’t count. That’s to be verified by big data. According to Baidu Index, the number of people who search for insomnia every day is about 3000-4000, and it has been so since 2011 and now 2022.
It can be seen that modern life is not easy. Due to various pressures, more and more people suffer from insomnia.
In order to confirm again whether there is a great demand for insomnia, search “insomnia” directly in Du Niang
Through the drop-down words, it is found that they are all the needs of poor sleep. What does this mean?
It’s very simple. It shows that a large number of people are searching for solutions in this field every day, otherwise they won’t appear in the drop-down box. Of course, if you want to solve insomnia, you must find a doctor. As ordinary people, of course, we are not doctors. Of course, we can’t solve the problem of insomnia for others. Most people think so. However, as marketers and entrepreneurs, we need more divergent thinking to consider. In fact, many times, we don’t need to completely solve the problem of insomnia. As long as we can alleviate it, there is a huge market.
There are many reasons for insomnia, such as cervical problems, promotion pressure, life difficulties, etc. insomnia caused by different reasons has given birth to corresponding products.
